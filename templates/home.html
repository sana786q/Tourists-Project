"Jabalpur is one of the most important cities in the State . It is situated in the 'Mahakaushal' region and gets its name from history,being the native land of the great 'Jabali Rishi' . Its scenic beauty made it a pleasure resort of the Gond Kings . Jabalpur is a city of growing commercial importance , through it is renowned for its scenic charms , in particular the magnificant Marble Rocks . Spread across the Vindhyan ranges , it's a beautifull paradise for archaeologists ang geologits . The rich chronicling lengend of Jabalpur is visible in various forts , monuments and temples , which are visible in different parts of the city and have now , become major tourist attractions of Jabalpur"
